# Acceptable Use

Rules for using the University Medicine Essen  Research Data system and its associated storage tiers. By signing in you agree to abide by this policy. 
Violations may result in credential revocation and an incident report being created.

## Permitted use

Storage of de-idenfitied patient derived clinical data covered by an approved ethics protocol (Ethik-Kommission) and non patient derived biomedical research 
Use of the S3 API for ingest, processing, and analysis pipelines that run inside or alongside the research data infrastructure.

##Prohibited use

Storage of patient-identifying data.
Sharing access keys between users; every credential must be tied to one application or person.
Storing or transmitting cryptocurrency-mining workloads, malware, copyright-violating content, or material that breaches the rights of third parties.
Bypassing rate limits, scraping logs, or circumventing the audit trail.
Re-identification of pseudonymized datasets outside the consented research purpose.

##Right to revoke

The reseaerch data cluster operations team may revoke credentials, suspend accounts, and remove data without prior notice when required to protect the integrity of the platform, comply with a lawful order, or address a violation of this policy. Affected users will be notified through the e-mail address on file.
