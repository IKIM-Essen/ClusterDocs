# Class 6 video plan: protected project websites

The Class 6 narration has been expanded into an 8–10 minute YouTube-ready recording script. Recording is intentionally deferred until novice testing and the rollout values are complete.

## Scope covered by the future video

- what a governed vhost is;
- four supported website patterns;
- what does not belong on the platform;
- hospital and external access in user-friendly terms;
- division of responsibility between RCC and the project team;
- safe database, file, upload and computational patterns;
- request, approval and deployment workflow;
- the copyable learner example and completion gate.

## Publication principle

The canonical ClusterDocs page remains authoritative. The video should provide orientation and explanation, while current URLs, policy details and commands remain on the maintained website.
